Deekshabhoomi

Deekshabhoomi is a sacred mo.ment where Dr. B.R. Ambedkar, the architect of the Indian Constitution, converted to Buddhism along with thousands of followers. It is a symbol of social and religious transformation and holds immense significance for followers of Buddhism worldwide.

Sitabuldi Fort

Sitabuldi Fort is a historic fort located in Nagpur. It is known for its strategic importance and the battles fought here during the Maratha era. The fort offers a panoramic view of the city and is a popular tourist attraction.

Ramtek Temple

Ramtek Temple is a famous Hindu temple located on a hilltop near Nagpur. It is dedicated to Lord Rama and offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape. The temple is an important pilgrimage site and a popular tourist destination.

Ambazari Lake

Ambazari Lake is one of the largest lakes in Nagpur, known for its scenic beauty and recreational activities. The lake is surrounded by lush greenery and is a popular spot for picnics and boating.
