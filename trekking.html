Trekking

Experience the scenic beauty and diverse terrain of Nagpur's trekking trails. From beginner-friendly paths to challenging hikes, there's a trail for every level.

Learn More

Seminary Hills

Seminary Hills offers stunning trails with panoramic views of Nagpur. It's a popular spot for both beginners and experienced trekkers.

Opening Hours: Open daily from 6:00 AM to 6:00 PM

Entry Fees: Free

Sitabuldi Fort

Located in the heart of Nagpur, Sitabuldi Fort provides historical charm along with trekking opportunities. Explore its rich history while enjoying the trekking trails.

Opening Hours: Open daily from 7:00 AM to 5:00 PM

Entry Fees: INR 20 per person

Ramtek Fort Temple

About 50 km from Nagpur, Ramtek Fort Temple combines trekking with spiritual exploration. The trek to the fort offers serene surroundings and a glimpse into history.

Opening Hours: Open daily from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM

Entry Fees: Free
